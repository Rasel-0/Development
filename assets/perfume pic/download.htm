Description

Terrible Teddy by Penhaligon’s: A Bold Leather Fragrance for the Modern Man

Launched in 2019, Terrible Teddy by Penhaligon’s is a fragrance that boldly embodies sophistication with a daring edge. This leather fragrance, created by renowned perfumer Quentin Bisch, is designed for the confident, assertive man who craves a scent that complements his strong personality. With a unique composition that blends smoky incense, rich leather, and a sensual base of ambroxan, this fragrance provides an unforgettable olfactory experience.

Notes: The Power of Incense and Leather

The top note of Terrible Teddy is incense, giving the fragrance an immediate smoky and mysterious aura. This sets the stage for the heart of the fragrance, which is dominated by leather—strong, smooth, and unmistakable. The base note of ambroxan adds a warm, musky, and slightly sweet finish, creating a long-lasting trail that lingers throughout the day.

Who is it for?

Terrible Teddy is perfect for the man who wants a fragrance that exudes both strength and elegance. It’s for those who appreciate classic, bold leather scents with a modern twist. Ideal for individuals who want to leave a lasting impression, this fragrance is not for the faint of heart.

When to Wear it?

This fragrance is versatile, making it suitable for both day and evening wear. However, due to its bold nature, it is best suited for colder seasons like fall and winter, when the rich, smoky, and leathery notes can shine through. The fragrance is perfect for formal occasions, important meetings, or a night out, where you want to stand out and leave a lasting impression.
